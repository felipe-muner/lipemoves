# LipeMoves — 1-Month Content Launch Strategy
### Calisthenics · Yoga · Kettlebell · Longevity

---

## Brand Identity

**Niche:** Functional movement for a longer, better life — not aesthetics, not ego lifting.  
**Tone:** Calm, grounded, educational. Real human, not a fitness robot.  
**Hook:** *"Move well. Live long."*  
**Target audience:** 25–45 yo professionals who feel stiff, stressed, and want sustainable fitness.  
**Differentiator:** The blend. Not pure yoga (too soft for some), not pure calisthenics (too hard for others). The intersection of all three — mobility + strength + breathwork — framed through longevity science.

## Weekend Batching System

**Record everything Saturday or Sunday. Post through the week.**

| Weekend task | Time |
|---|---|
| Film 3 reels (back to back, change outfit between) | 2h |
| Film 1 raw talking-head for TikTok | 20min |
| Film B-roll (hands, feet, equipment close-ups) | 30min |
| Edit reels in CapCut | 1.5h |
| Build 2 carousels in Canva | 1h |
| Write all captions + hashtags | 30min |
| Schedule everything in Later/Buffer | 20min |
| **Total** | **~6h per weekend** |

Then during the week: **stories daily (5 min)** + **engagement daily (20 min)**. That's it.

## Reel Formula That Works

```
[0-3s]  HOOK — Say/show something that stops the scroll
         "Most people destroy their knees doing this..."
         "I trained every day for 30 days and here's what happened..."

[3-20s] VALUE — The actual drill, tip, or teaching

[20-28s] SOFT CTA — "Follow for more" or "Save this for later"
```

**Captions:**
- Line 1 = hook (repeat the video hook in text)
- 3-5 lines of value
- Call to action at the bottom
- 5-8 hashtags (not 30) — niche-specific

## Stories System (daily)

Stories are your relationship layer. Use them daily even when you don't post a feed post.

- **Morning:** A thought, a question, a poll
- **Training:** Real clip from today's session (raw, unedited is fine)
- **Evening:** Tip of the day or a screenshot of something you learned

Use **link stickers** once you hit 10k (or now with a Linktree link).

## Engagement Rules (non-negotiable first 30 days)

1. **Reply to every comment within 2 hours** of posting
2. **Leave 10 genuine comments per day** on accounts in your niche (not "great post!")
3. **DM 3 new followers per day** with a simple thank you + question
4. **Watch and comment on competitors' Reels** — their audience sees you
5. **Share others' content to stories** and tag them — they'll notice

## Platform Strategy

### Instagram — PRIMARY (start here)
- Best for visual movement content
- Reels algorithm still rewards new creators
- Best monetization path: paid programs, brand deals, digital products
- **Goal month 1:** 500 real followers, 100 saves per carousel

### TikTok — SECONDARY (repurpose from IG)
- Upload same reels (remove watermark — use CapCut or Canva)
- TikTok pushes new accounts harder than IG
- Can blow up a single video to 100k views
- **Start week 2** once you have 3-4 Instagram reels already made

### YouTube Shorts — TERTIARY (repurpose same content)
- Slowest growth but best for long-term SEO
- When you have 10+ Shorts, start doing 10-15 min long-form tutorials
- YouTube pays the best per 1000 views once monetized
- **Start week 3**

### Priority order: Instagram → TikTok → YouTube Shorts → YouTube Long-form

## Monetization Roadmap

### Month 1-3: Build the asset
- No selling yet. Build trust and audience.
- Collect emails via a free lead magnet (e.g. "7-day morning mobility PDF")
- Use Beehiiv or ConvertKit for email list — **this is your real asset**

### Month 3-6: First revenue
| Product | Price | Platform |
|---|---|---|
| 4-week mobility program (PDF/video) | $27-47 | Gumroad / Lemon Squeezy |
| 1:1 online coaching (limited slots) | $150-300/mo | DM → Calendly → Stripe |
| Group coaching / community | $30-50/mo | Skool or Whop |

### Month 6-12: Scale
| Channel | How |
|---|---|
| **Brand deals** | Reach out to mobility tools, supplements, mats brands |
| **YouTube AdSense** | After 1k subs + 4k watch hours |
| **Digital courses** | $97-197 self-paced program |
| **Affiliate** | Amazon storefront, VivoBareFoot, Perform Better |

### Realistic income projections (conservative)
- Month 3: $0-200 (first PDF sales)
- Month 6: $500-1,500/mo
- Month 12: $2,000-5,000/mo (if consistent)
