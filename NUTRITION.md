# Nutrition Plan — Felipe Muner (72kg → 75kg)
### Training: Yoga · Calisthenics · Gym (free weights) · Kettlebell

---

## Your Numbers

| | Now | Target |
|---|---|---|
| **Bodyweight** | 72 kg | 75 kg |
| **Goal** | Lean bulk — +3kg muscle | ~10–16 weeks at 0.25–0.5kg/week |
| **Daily calories** | ~3,300–3,500 kcal | Surplus of ~400–500 kcal above TDEE |
| **Protein** | 175–200g (2.4–2.8g/kg) | Keep high throughout bulk |
| **Carbs** | 380–420g (5.3–5.8g/kg) | Main lever for gaining weight |
| **Fats** | 75–90g | Don't drop below 70g |

> **To gain 3kg lean:** you need a consistent caloric surplus every day — not just on training days. The extra calories go here: more rice, more oats, more banana, more olive oil. Don't add junk.

> **Timeline:** 0.25–0.5kg/week gain is the sweet spot. Faster = fat, not muscle. At 0.3kg/week → 75kg in ~10 weeks.

---

## The Rules

- **Protein at every meal** — never a meal without a source
- **Carbs around training** — front-load before, replenish after
- **Don't skip fats** — hormones (testosterone), joint health, recovery
- **Hydration** — 3–3.5L water/day minimum (more on training days)
- **Eat within 30–60 min after training** — non-negotiable for your volume

## Supplements (what actually matters)

| Supplement | Dose | When |
|---|---|---|
| Creatine monohydrate | 5g | Any time, daily — with water |
| Whey protein | 1 scoop (25–30g) | Post-training if you miss food |
| Vitamin D3 | 2,000–4,000 IU | Morning with food |
| Magnesium glycinate | 300mg | Night — helps sleep + recovery |
| Omega-3 (fish oil) | 2–3g EPA/DHA | With any meal |

> Creatine is the only supplement with proven muscle/strength gains. Don't skip it.

## Tracking (first 4 weeks)

Track everything on **MyFitnessPal** or **Cronometer** for the first month. After that you'll know your portions by eye.

**Weekly check:** weigh yourself every Monday morning, fasted.
- Gaining 0–0.5kg/week → perfect lean bulk
- Gaining more → slightly reduce carbs at dinner
- Not gaining → add 200 kcal (extra rice + olive oil)
