# Nutrition Plan — Fasting Protocol (72kg → 75kg)
### Eating window: 5:00 PM – 9:30 PM · Training fasted · 4.5h window

---

## The Protocol

| | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Style** | 20:4 Intermittent Fasting (OMAD-adjacent) |
| **Fast window** | 9:30 PM → 5:00 PM (19.5h fast) |
| **Eating window** | 5:00 PM → 9:30 PM (4.5h) |
| **Goal calories** | ~3,200–3,400 kcal in the window |
| **Protein** | 180–210g crammed into 3 meals |
| **Carbs** | 380–420g |
| **Fats** | 80–100g |

> **Honest note:** Gaining muscle on one eating window is harder than spreading across 5 meals — but it works if you hit your numbers every single day. The bigger challenge is eating 3,300 kcal in 4.5h without feeling like shit. It's doable. You'll get used to it in 2–3 weeks.

---

## During the Fast (Morning → 5 PM)

These do NOT break your fast and keep you functional for training:

| What | Why |
|---|---|
| Water | Unlimited |
| Black coffee | Fine — may suppress appetite (good) |
| Sparkling water | Fine |
| Electrolytes (no sugar) | Salt, potassium, magnesium — prevents fatigue |
| Green tea | Fine |

**Avoid during fast:** anything with calories — no BCAAs, no protein shakes, no juice, no milk in coffee.

## Training Fasted — What to Know

Training on an empty stomach with your volume (yoga + calisthenics + kettlebell) is intense. Here's how to manage it:

| Situation | Solution |
|---|---|
| Feel weak/dizzy during training | Add a pinch of salt + squeeze of lemon to water |
| Low energy before session | 2 espressos 30 min before — legal performance boost |
| Light-headed during kettlebell | Eat a small date or 2 (20g carbs) — minimal fast impact |
| Post-training hunger before 5pm | Black coffee + 1L water — hold the window |

> Best training window fasted: **morning or early afternoon**. The closer to 5pm you train, the easier it is to hold the fast and eat immediately after.

## Meal 1 — Break Fast (5:00 PM)

**First thing into the body after ~20h. Start with carbs + protein. Not too fatty yet.**

| Food | Amount | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| White rice (cooked) | 350g | 6g | 77g | 0g |
| Chicken breast (grilled) | 250g | 55g | 0g | 6g |
| Banana | 2 medium | 2g | 54g | 0g |
| Olive oil (on rice) | 1 tbsp | 0g | 0g | 14g |
| Cherry tomatoes + cucumber | 1 cup | 2g | 8g | 0g |

**How:** eat slow, chew well. Your stomach is empty — don't inhale this. Give 10 min between the banana and the rice/chicken.

## Meal 3 — Night Close (9:00 PM)

**Last call. High protein, moderate carbs, enough fat to slow digestion overnight.**

| Food | Amount | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Greek yogurt (full fat) | 300g | 30g | 15g | 9g |
| Cottage cheese | 150g | 17g | 5g | 3g |
| Oats (cooked, warm) | 80g | 11g | 54g | 5g |
| Honey | 1 tbsp | 0g | 17g | 0g |
| Walnuts or almonds | 25g | 5g | 5g | 13g |

> Greek yogurt + cottage cheese at night = slow-digesting protein (casein-like). Feeds muscle recovery while you sleep. This matters a lot when you're not eating for 20h.

**Alternative night close if not hungry enough for oats:**
- 400g Greek yogurt + 30g whey mixed in + walnuts
- Easier to eat when full, still hits ~55g protein

## Supplements — Timing for Fasting Protocol

| Supplement | When | Note |

| Vitamin D3 | With Meal 1 | Fat-soluble — needs food to absorb |

| Whey (optional) | If Meal 3 feels low on protein | Only if you're under 180g on the day |

## Progress Check

Weigh every Monday, same time, fasted (before 5pm).

| Weekly scale result | Action |
|---|---|
| +0.2 to +0.5kg | Perfect — keep going |
| No change after 2 weeks | Add 200 kcal — more rice at Meal 1 + Meal 2 |
| +0.7kg or more | Drop 150 kcal — reduce rice by 50g |
| Feeling depleted, weak | Add electrolytes during fast + 1 date pre-training |

## The Hard Part

The first 10–14 days your body will push back. Normal side effects:

- **Hunger spikes at noon** → coffee + 1L water → goes away
- **Low energy during training** → electrolytes + train closer to 5pm while adapting
- **Hard to eat 3,300 kcal in window** → add calorie-dense foods (see cheat codes above)
- **Waking up hungry at night** → increase Meal 3, especially the protein/fat

After 2 weeks your hunger hormones (ghrelin) re-sync to the new window. It gets noticeably easier.
