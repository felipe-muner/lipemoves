## Phase 1 — Content Machine (Month 1-2)

**Goal: 50 paying subscribers**

Execute your weekend batch system:

- Saturday: film 3 reels + 1 talking head + B-roll
- Sunday: edit in CapCut, write captions, schedule in Buffer
- Weekdays: stories daily (5 min) + reply to every single comment

**Hook formula that converts:**
```
"[Specific problem] in [time frame] — here's the exact drill"
Example: "Kill lower back pain in 5 minutes — this hip flow is why"
```

**CTA on every video:** "Full 30-day program inside LipeMoves — link in bio"

**When someone DMs "interested"** → personally reply, send them the link, ask what their main struggle is → that's your first 50 customers

## Phase 2 — Paid Acquisition (Month 3-4)

**Goal: R$10K/mo MRR (~100 subscribers)**

- **$20/day Meta ads** — promote your top 3 organic videos as ads
- **Target:** Brazil + Portugal, age 28-42, interests: yoga, wellness, stress, home workout
- **Funnel:** Ad → Landing page → Free challenge → Email sequence (7 days) → Pitch subscription
- **Email sequence:**
  - Day 1: Welcome + Day 1 flow video
  - Day 3: Tip
  - Day 5: "You're halfway"
  - Day 7: "Here's what's next inside LipeMoves" + offer

Track **CPA (cost per acquisition)**. If CPA < R$50 on a R$97/mo subscription, scale budget.
